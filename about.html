About the Event

The Roof of Africa started in 1967 and has become one of the most famous off-road motorcycle races in the world. It is held annually in Lesotho and is known as the "Mother of Hard Enduro".

Importance of the Event

  • Promotes tourism in Lesotho
  • Attracts international riders and spectators
  • Showcases Lesotho's mountains and landscapes
  • Supports the local economy

Target Tourists

  • Motorcycle racing fans
  • Adventure tourists
  • International sports tourists
  • Local spectators
